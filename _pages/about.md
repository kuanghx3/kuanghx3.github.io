## 👋 About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the **School of Intelligent Systems Engineering**, Sun Yat-sen University (中山大学), and a member of the **Guangdong Provincial Key Laboratory of Intelligent Transportation System**. My research focuses on applying **deep learning** and **spatio-temporal data mining** techniques to tackle challenges in intelligent transportation systems, particularly in electric vehicle (EV) charging demand prediction, parking occupancy forecasting, and urban mobility analysis.

My work has been published in top-tier journals including *Applied Energy* (IF=12.2), *Sustainable Cities and Society* (IF=13.3), *Energy* (IF=10.1), *Advanced Engineering Informatics* (IF=11.5), and *IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems*.

---

## 🔬 Research Interests

- ⚡ **Electric Vehicle Charging Demand Prediction** — Spatio-temporal modeling of citywide EV charging behavior
- 💰 **Energy Economics & Pricing** — Price elasticity of demand, dynamic pricing strategies for EV charging
- 🅿️ **Parking Occupancy Prediction** — Deep learning with heterogeneous information fusion
- 🚕 **Urban Mobility & Taxi Distribution** — Impact of ride-hailing platforms on traditional transportation
- 🧠 **Deep Learning** — Graph Neural Networks (GCN, GAT, Hypergraph), Transformer, LSTM, Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINN), Meta-Learning
- 🏙️ **Smart City & Intelligent Transportation Systems**
